## Severity Classification

### SEV1 – System Critical
- Service unavailable or fully broken
- Data loss or corruption
- No workaround
- Broad customer impact

### SEV2 – High Impact
- Core functionality impaired
- Multiple users impacted
- Workaround exists but is not viable long-term

### SEV3 – Medium Impact
- Partial feature failure
- Limited user impact
- Workaround exists

### SEV4 – Low Impact
- Cosmetic / UI issues
- Questions or configuration help
- No functional impact
